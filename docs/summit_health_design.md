## Pharmacy Department
Summit Health Care has 1 centralized inpatient pharmacy supporting all hospital locations, 1 outpatient pharmacy, and 1 specialty pharmacy.

Pharmacy operations include:
    - Central pharmacy
    - IV sterile compounding
    - Decentral clinical pharmacy (ICU, medsurg, emergency department, oncology)
    - Automated dispensing cabinets (Omnicell)
    - Medication safety services
    - Controlled substance management
    - Pharmacy purchasing
